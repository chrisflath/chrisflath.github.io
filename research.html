Research

My research program explores the intersection of operations management, microeconomics, and business analytics. I focus on analyzing large-scale data—from sensor streams and transaction logs to web-scraped data—and combining these insights with optimization approaches to build data-driven decision support systems.

Business Analytics & Machine Learning

We develop advanced analytics and machine learning methods to transform raw data into actionable business insights. Our work spans predictive modeling, deep learning applications, and the design of intelligent decision support systems.

A recent highlight is deepflash2, a deep learning framework for bioimage segmentation published in Nature Communications. This tool enables researchers to accurately segment ambiguous microscopy images using state-of-the-art neural networks.

Key Topics

  • Deep learning for image analysis
  • Predictive analytics in business
  • Automated decision support systems
  • Fashion supply chain analytics

Featured Publication

  • Nature Communications (2023): Deepflash2

Smart Grids & Energy Systems

The transition to sustainable energy systems requires intelligent coordination of distributed energy resources. My research designs market mechanisms and optimization approaches for electric vehicle charging, demand response, and grid integration of renewables.

We develop pricing mechanisms that align individual incentives with system-wide efficiency goals, enabling scalable coordination without centralized control.

Key Topics

  • Electric vehicle charging coordination
  • Area pricing mechanisms
  • Demand response optimization
  • Battery switching stations

Featured Publication

  • Transportation Science (2014): EV Charging

Platform Economics & Privacy

Digital platforms have transformed how we consume, work, and interact. My research examines the economic implications of platform-mediated transactions, with particular attention to privacy considerations in data-driven business models.

We study how platform design affects user behavior, market outcomes, and welfare—including the often-overlooked dimension of privacy in the sharing economy.

Key Topics

  • Sharing economy dynamics
  • Privacy valuation and trade-offs
  • Platform design and incentives
  • Data-driven business models

Featured Publication

  • JAIS (2019): Privacy in Sharing Economy
